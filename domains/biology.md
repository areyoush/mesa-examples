# Biology Examples

Examples that model biological systems, processes, and interactions at individual or population levels.

---

### Hexagonal Ant Foraging
An agent-based model of ant foraging behavior on a hexagonal grid where agents explore the environment, deposit pheromone trails, and collectively form emergent paths to food sources.

**Example:**  
[examples/hex_ant](../examples/hex_ant)

---

### Termite Woodchip Behaviour
A decentralized coordination model where termite agents move randomly and interact with wood chips using simple pickup and drop rules, producing emergent pile formation.

**Example:**  
[examples/termites](../examples/termites)

---

### Virus-Antibody Model
A model of immune response dynamics where virus and antibody agents interact in continuous space, demonstrating mutation, adaptation, and collective defense behavior.

**Example:**  
[examples/virus_antibody](../examples/virus_antibody)
