# Economics Examples

Examples that model markets, decision-making, wealth distribution, and strategic behavior in economic systems.

---

### Bank Reserves Model
A simplified model of a monetary economy where agents move randomly and engage in probabilistic trade, generating deposits, withdrawals, and loans within a centralized banking system.

**Example:**  
[examples/bank_reserves](../examples/bank_reserves)

---

### Boltzmann Wealth Model (Network)
An agent-based model of wealth exchange on a network where agents randomly transfer money to neighbors, producing emergent wealth inequality consistent with statistical mechanics principles.

**Example:**  
[examples/boltzmann_wealth_model_network](../examples/boltzmann_wealth_model_network)

---

### El Farol Bar Model
A model of bounded rationality where agents decide whether to attend a bar based on past attendance and prediction strategies, producing fluctuating collective behavior around capacity limits.

**Example:**  
[examples/el_farol](../examples/el_farol)

---

### Hotelling's Law Simulation
An agent-based implementation of Hotelling's spatial competition model where firms adjust location and price to maximize market share in a linear market.

**Example:**  
[examples/hotelling_law](../examples/hotelling_law)
